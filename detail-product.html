24in. Promo Banner Stand

$79

The Promo Stand offers a cost effective option for retail or trade show applications. The aluminum center pole adjusts from 36" - 72" tall, giving you maximum flexibility in both size and design. And every stand can handle one or two graphics for a single or double-sided application!

Our standard vinyl graphic is printed on a smooth 14oz. scrim vinyl. For even better print quality, colors and durability, choose our high-resolution 16oz. vinyl graphic.Either way, you'll get a stylish, attractive and versatile display at a terrific price. Graphics are unsewn for a crisp, clean look.
